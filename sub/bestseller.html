Backrest

Earth Stool is a circular bar stool designed by Danish
architect Eva Harlou. Earth Stool is the result of a new
innovative waste-to-value technique that enables Mater
to reuse companies’ industrial waste, e.g. plastic waste
and other fibre-based materials.
The seats are made from upcycled industrial plastic
waste from GROHE and coffee bean shells from BKI
coffee. The technique is developed in collaboration with
Danish Technological Institute with support from the
University of Copenhagen.
